About Me

My name is Cameron Ythan King. I attend school at Little Red Schoolhouse and Elisabeth Irwin High School (LREI) , located in SoHo, New York. I am a Prep for Prep and All Star Code alum. I live in Brooklyn, New York. I consider myself an athelte, a musician, a comedian, an artist, a designer and I am an aspiring Computer Engineer. I was LREI's Athlete of the Year in 2015.

I traveled to France on a scholarship with the Student Diplomacy Corps I recently represented LREI at the White Privilege Conference in Louisville, Kentucky and help led a Black Lives Matter protest with my school. This summer, I participated in New York City's "Ladders for Leaders" program. I am an intern at Bleu Magazine, a men's fashion magazine. I am building their mobile application. I represented ASC at their annual benefit and I was elected to be an ASC ambassador. I was also iniated into the Young Hackers organization; an exclusive group of teenagers who organize hackathons. I created the website for NYC Local Hack-Day and managed the social media, branding, and marketing of the event.
